Let’s hand in hand to give more love in Makalot Initial Charity Year!

2017 is a specifically meaningful year when Makalot encouraged every department to take turns in hosting a charity event, allowing more employees to have the opportunity to participate in charity, so as to understand the idea of it is more blessed to give than to receive. Makalot cooperated with the following charity groups and held 16 activities. While Makalot volunteers paid a lot of effort when they attended the voluntary activities, they could feel so much about love and gain a lot when serving for the people who need help.

  • 捐血中心Blood Center

TFCF - Makalot babies program

Makalot and employees join together to adopt children from domestic and overseas disadvantaged families, and provide every child NT$700 to NT$1,000 on a monthly basis. To offer more quality assistance to these families, the Company would subsidize half of the donation when employees take part in the adoption. Makalot employees who are interested in sponsoring children from disadvantaged families need not make remittance arrangements. The Company would deduct the donation amount directly from salaries, providing a convenient approach for employees to take part in charity.

Growing with Makalot program - CCSA X Lohas Preschool X Makalot

Makalot has cooperated with CCSA for a long time and arranged volunteer activities to interact with children in CCSA, hoping to encourage and care for them through those activities. In 2016, Makalot employees volunteered to take children of Lohas Preschool to participate in environmental protection activities organized by SOW. We are deeply aware that the cooperation with the children’s home must be cultivated. Therefore, the Lohas Preschool will be a Makalot’s long-term partner in the future. In 2017, to help children understand the importance of self-reliance in their future, the Preschool and Makalot organized the “Growing up Happily with Makalot” program where volunteers organized one-day trip with the children. During the process, the children interacted with volunteers by discussing activity details and learned how to arrange, communicate and execute tasks. We hope that the sense of achievement from those trips would give children more confidence and courage in actively exploring the outside world, and thus have a smooth transition into the society.

Meal box adoption - Taiwan Chief Executive Officer Club for Social Benefit

Makalot purchased and donated 200 boxes to the “new poor” families with disadvantaged children and orphans. The meal box contains healthy and safe ingredients including toxic-free Siluo Rice, seasonal fruits and vegetables and meats, which have been farmed through organic or pesticide-free methods by small farmers in Yunlin.。

Blood donation day - Taipei Blood Center

Blood donation is Makalot’s first charity activity and has been held for ten years since 2017. There are 2 to 3 blood donation events organized each year where Makalot employees and community members have always participated with enthusiasm. The amount of blood donated has hit record high several times.

Vietnam

Aid for impoverished/ill family members of employees

The factory launches activities to assist disadvantages employees on its own accord, providing living expenses and goods to employees from impoverished families or with ill family members. The factory would take the initiative of maintaining a list of employees from impoverished families or with ill family members within the factory. The factory management would then visit and deliver goods to the abovementioned employees and their families in October, which is set to be the month of caring. In addition, the factory provides consumer durables (e.g. TVs/washing machines, etc.) equivalent to VND 5 million to those employees in need.

Scholarships for students from low income families

Besides employees' children, as a contribution to the society, the factory donated VND 10 million in 2017 as scholarships, encouraging outstanding students not to allow financial difficulties at home to interfere their studies.

Philippines

Assistance to community schools and disadvantaged groups

Local production sites/factories have set up charity funds, helping local community schools and public welfare organizations. Actions taken include the donation of desks and chairs to local schools and the donation of food and personal hygiene products to the elderly living alone.

  • We also assist schools with building campuses, donate desks and chairs, and arrange volunteer activities to interact directly with local children.
  • Apart from donating food and personal hygiene products to homes for the elderly, volunteer activities are also arranged to accompany and entertain elders who lost their relatives.

Cambodia

Assistance to disabled children and families

We visit local disabled students and their families, and provide school materials and supplies to help them improve their basic learning and living conditions.
