Overseas Employee Career Development
  • P.A.C.E. (Personal Advancement and Career Enhancement)
  • It is an education program collaborated and planned by Makalot and its customer, GAP, since 2010 and designed specifically for female employees in countries of production. The oversea factory management would attend the GAP training workshops first and then teach factory employees as lecturers. The program includes literacy, health awareness, time and stress management, as well as basic concepts to wealth management.
  • Makalot initiated this project at the Indonesian production site. Since 2015, it has expanded to two major production sites of Vietnam and Cambodia. The Company hopes that this positive effect can benefit more female employees. The goal is to have approximately 7 thousand female workers joining P.A.C.E. program by 2020.
  • Participants of P.A.C.E. program would learn how to communicate with factory managers, and how to manage their own time properly and deal with stress, as well as receive health and sanitation education and wealth management knowledge, etc. Makalot wishes that all employees can have more confidence in themselves and enjoy their work.
