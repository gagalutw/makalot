Stakeholders' Topics of Concern
Engagement Process

In an attempt to identify the issues that matter most to our business and our stakeholders and to examine the effectiveness of the promotion of sustainability, Makalot identifies material issues through materiality analysis following the GRI Standards and the AA 1000SES standards. We undergo the following six steps:

Set up Communication Channels

In efforts to make disclosed information within the report in line with the expectations of stakeholders, we utilize daily communication channels with the eight types of stakeholders to get a better understanding of their concerns and in turn give relevant responses through this CSR report.

Identification of material issues

In 2016, Makalot introduced the analysis of material issues into the CSR report. Those ranking high in importance on both the X-axis of the matrix "the degree of impact on the sustainable development of Makalot" and the Y-axis "the degree of interest of stakeholders" are categorized as material issues, with a total of 15.
