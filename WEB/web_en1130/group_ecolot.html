Ecolot Textile

Ecolot Textile, registered in 2011, is a fabric sourcing institution focused on Taiwan , China and Vietnam materials. The establishment of Ecolot Textile is to further strengthen multiple geographical sourcing advantage and fulfill material localization in order to offer the excellent pricing and saving delivery time to our clients.

Localization

Ecolot sources various kind of materials from China, Taiwan, and Vietnam. We reserve huge local capacity to fulfill customer's multi-nation production arrangement. It will not only save time on material transit but is beneficial to reduce the expense.

Flexibility

Ecolot delivers flexible service and we source for a wide range of products include Ready Active Wear, Sportswear, Ready to wear, Sleepwear in multiple regions. We provide efficient communication in between customers and local mills for a smooth development & production.

Price Competitiveness

Ecolot set up strong and firm relationship with major suppliers through seasonal order projection. With our in-depth material forward market observation, our collaborated suppliers can regulate yarn booking at the relatively low level cost to minimize influence from price fluctuation.
