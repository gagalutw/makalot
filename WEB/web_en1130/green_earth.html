Development of eco-friendly textile products

Makalot is considering the increased environmental awareness among consumers and the emphasis on global warming related issues. Makalot continues developing fabrics from recycled polyester filament and organic materials to make products more eco-friendly and reduce chemical waste. For sportswear product, Makalot focus on dope-dyed yarn and digital printing developments which reduce dyestuff and wastewater since 2016. By end of 2017, Makalot committed to use over 200 tons of dye-less fabric with estimated 800,000 liters water saving and reduce 400,000 kg of CO2 emission. Makalot is considering functional developments such as permanent wicking, anti-bacterial and anti-odor by using chemical free methods to reduce synthesis consumption and lower the impact to the environment.

Green building LEED Certificate – Makalot Headquarter has got Gold certificate in Oct. 2016
  • We schemed the office layout plan with LEED-CI evaluation system and have got the Gold certificate in Oct. 2016.
  • We can provide employees with more comfortable and eco-friendly working environment by reducing energy consumption (saving electricity and water), using environmental material and facility, controlling the usage of chemical, pollutant and etc.
Promoting environmental protection in office
  • Advocating energy conservation to reduce the waste of resources.
  • Makalot regularly shares knowledge about energy conservation and carbon reduction with all its employees.
Zero carbon emission – tree planting plan

Besides reducing carbon emission by following LEED standard, we have started cooperating with Tse-Sin Organic Agriculture Foundation in 2016 and set up the goal to plant 7,500 trees in 2020 so as to offset the carbon emission caused by Makalot headquarters. We have planted more than 2,000 trees and invite employees to join the one day volunteer activity to help plant trees and protect trees with sticks till now.

Makalot green partner – The Society of Wilderness

In early 2007, Makalot launched cooperation with The Society of Wilderness by carrying out beach cleanup activities. Makalot encouraged all its employees and their families to participate, so that they can learn more about related environmental protection knowledge.

  • In 2014, chemical food additives which may risk people’s health caused serious food safety issues. We are aware that we should have different actions to help people take healthier food. Therefore, we decide to do it from our staple food – Rice. Makalot starts focusing our donation to S.O.W. on the farmland to use the way of “Natural farming”. Through the volunteer activities, let our employees and families know the negative impact of traditional farming method. We are also excited that we can have “Makalot Rice” which is cultivated in Yi-Lan Ma-Sai and donated the income to local charity project to support the education of teenager from disadvantage family.
  • Since 2015, we have taken kids from CCSA to join the one day voluntary activity cooperated with S.O.W. This is with more environmental educational value instead of only for fun.
  • In 2017, Makalot cooperates with 104 Job Bank for the project - An9. More people can join the donation activity to support the conservation plan of 52-Jia Wetland led by S.O.W. on 104 An9 website platform.
