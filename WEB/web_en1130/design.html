Trends and Design
Firm Grasp of Global Trends

Makalot is dedicated to providing trendy and diverse clothing. Precise grasp of fashion trends and Makalot’s ability to adapt have been the key to the company’s success. To fulfill the needs of clients across US, Europe and Asia, the Makalot design team constantly gathers the latest fashion trend information from fashion capitals such as Paris, London and New York, which is then integrated and analyzed to provide Makalot’s clients with the most comprehensive and systematic market, material, and design intelligence.

Accurate & Customized Service

Different clients and product lines are unique in their own right, and to achieve a comprehensive customized design service, Makalot must plan in accordance with different product properties. In order to fulfill various people’s needs, Makalot has developed clothing for men, women and children, and the range of products covers ready-to-wear clothing, sleepwear, active wear and even functional and active performance products, such as fireproof wear, recycle fabric, heat fabric etc.

Systematic Information

In addition to trend analysis, market surveys, materials sourcing and customized service, Makalot has been dedicated to enhance its service and information integration. Makalot joined the Innovative Technology Applications and Services Program of the Department of Industrial Technology, ROC Ministry of Economic Affairs, in 2009. In this program, the company’s internal information is integrated to create a business information platform, sample library, fabric library and PD&D project management system. By building up the systematic management system, Makalot not only enhances its service quality and efficiency, but has also brought some important innovative management concepts into work. The program was given the honor of outstanding rating in 2011 and once again received the “Innovation Applied Service Award” in 2012.
